Who Am I?

Kyle Cheung is a an aspiring Full Stack Developer, specializing in Front-End Development.

He is experienced in retouching, mobile, web and user experience design. He has the abiliy to apply and learn new skills on the spot. A jack of all trades, he is skilled in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as well as other back-end tools such as PHP and MySQL.

He is driven, passionate, and committed to producing great designs and positive user experiences. He lives to broaden his understanding of development principles and to utilize the knowledge by creating profound connections with a variety of audiences. Detail oriented, he thrives on learning about new innovative methods.
